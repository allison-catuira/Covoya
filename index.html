MEET THE TEAM

You'll find the word "collaboration" used a lot by our team members – but not because our name reflects the idea that coffee is a collaborative voyage. Rather, we chose the name Covoya because collaboration was already at the center of everything we do. It's in our DNA, almost a part of the air that we breathe: with our suppliers, our customers and each other. It's a journey that moves beyond the transactional and becomes a true investment in the success of our partners in both directions on the supply chain.
